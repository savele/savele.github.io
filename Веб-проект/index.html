THE ALLIANCE OF INNOVATION AND TRADITION

Code 11.59 by Audemars Piguet Tour Billon Openworked

This limited edition of 50 pieces encompasses a contemporary two-tone case interweaving 18-carat white gold with blue ceramic. The blue openworked bridges and main plate, visible on both sides of the watch, echo the colour of the case middle.

SIZE 41 mm THICKNESS 10.7 mm WATER RESISTANCE 30 m

CASE - Blue ceramic case with 18-carat white gold bezel and lugs, glareproofed sapphire crystal and caseback.
GLASS - The Manufacture has created a contemporary double curved sapphire crystal, whose tense, arched profile plays with depth, perspective and light for a unique optic experience. While its internal surface is shaped like a dome, it is vertically curved from 6 to 12 o’clock.
